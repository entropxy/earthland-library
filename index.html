What's inside

A full toolkit for
serious creative work.


01.

Project Pages

Every project gets a home — a living page you can build, update, and share. Not a portfolio. A workspace.

02.

Creator Profiles

One unified presence linking your work across every platform. YouTube, Substack, GitHub — all in one place.

03.

Clubs

Gather around a shared interest. Study groups, genre communities, creative crews — organized around the work.

04.

Branching Story Editor

Co-write, branch off each other's work, fork narratives. The collaborative tools actually built for writers.

05.

Programs & Resources

Structured creative development. Not just a feed — a place to learn, grow, and level up your craft.

06.

GitHub Integration

Every project optionally backed by a real repository. Version-controlled creative work, from day one.

How it works

From idea to
collaboration.

1

Create your profile

Link your existing work. Tell the Library what you make, what you're building, and who you're looking for.

2

Open a project

Every piece of work gets a page. Set it to private, invite-only, or open for collaboration.

3

Find your people

Browse clubs, join projects, or get matched with collaborators who make what you make.

4

Build something

Use the tools. Co-write, branch, fork, publish. The construction is the story.
